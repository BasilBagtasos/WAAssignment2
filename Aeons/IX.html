H.S.R Aeons

IX Nihility

IX.

IX is an apathetic Aeon, showing little interest in humanity and believing existence is meaningly. IX does not interact with the other Aeons and overall existance remains a mystery. It is recorded that being near IX's form can make one feel "numbed" and "dejected". Due to their belief of nothingness of the multiverse, not much is known on IX.

IX's appearance resembles a large purple blob with two white eyes, a large assortment of asteroids and planets being pulled towards them. What is considered their body is a spinning nebula of stars.

IX represents the path of Nihility.

Nihility

It is said those who follow the path of Nihility admire laziness, exhaustion and meaningless behavior. Unfortunately, there is not much to say.
