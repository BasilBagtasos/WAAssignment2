Yaoshi

Yaoshi, with their benevolent disposition, bestows their blessing upon those who seek immortality. However, the outcomes of these blessings often lead to fates more dire than death.

In appearance, Yaoshi is depicted as a figure with six arms and a scorpion-like hooked tail. Their hair resembles the feathers of a magpie, while antler-like branches sprout from their head. Their eyes are described as red slits with black sclera, and they are often depicted wielding a staff in Foxian culture

Yaoshi had gifted the Xianzhou the Ambrosial Arbor after they seeked out on a voyage to find Yaoshi in hopes they would give the Xianzhou immortality. However, the Amrbosial Arbor came to the Xianzhou almost as a curse as it lead the people under its effects susceptible to "Mara", a disease like effect that would destroy the Xianzhou internally and transform them into beatsts
